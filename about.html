About Scalex Global LLP

Scalex Global LLP is a Bengaluru-based startup focused on water treatment and scaling solutions. Our team specializes in designing, implementing, and maintaining systems that prioritize water conservation and operational efficiency.

We design projects that use scale removers and preventive strategies to reduce downtime and maintenance costs. Our approach is data-driven and tailored to each client's process requirements.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, reliable and scalable water treatment solutions that help industries and communities conserve water and reduce environmental impact.

Approach

We focus on pragmatic engineering: assessment, design, implementation and lifecycle support. Our solutions prioritize water conservation, regulatory compliance and operational uptime.

Assessment

Site surveys and sampling to identify scale sources.

Design

Tailored systems and chemical dosing strategies.

Implementation

Installation, commissioning and operator training.
